Overview

The physical foundation behind stable connectivity.

Network cabling is more than pulling cable from one point to another. The quality of routing, labeling, patching, and organization affects performance, maintenance, troubleshooting, and how scalable the space remains over time.

Arotech approaches cabling with an emphasis on structured layouts, neat installation, and practical long-term usability for offices, retail spaces, warehouses, and residential projects.

What this service can include

  • CAT5e, CAT6, and CAT6A installations
  • Data drops, workstation runs, and patching
  • Patch panel termination and cable labeling
  • Cleaner routing to support future maintenance

Why it matters

Better cabling reduces the mess and instability that often show up after poorly planned installs.

Where it fits

Ideal for offices, retail, production spaces, clinics, and homes that need stronger wired connectivity.

What you can expect

Cleaner cable management, steadier connectivity, and a more professional finished space.

Do you plan the cable routes first?

Yes. Layout and routing decisions are part of getting a cleaner result.

Is this only for businesses?

No. Residential projects can also benefit from structured and more reliable cabling.

Can this be expanded?

Yes. Planning with growth in mind helps make future changes easier to manage.
